Customer Purchase Behavior & Lead Generation Dashboard

An end-to-end business intelligence project analyzing customer purchasing behavior to identify high-value sales leads. From demographic overviews to an actionable Top 20 Lead List designed to optimize marketing investments.

Power BI DAX Excel Data Segmentation

Project Description

The Problem

The company faced low sales and an inefficient marketing strategy. Without a clear understanding of their ideal customer profile, the sales team could not prioritize leads, leading to wasted resources. The core challenge was: where should the company invest its next marketing campaign for the highest ROI?

The Solution

A multi-page interactive Power BI dashboard that transformed raw Excel data into a strategic roadmap. By cross-referencing demographic data with historical purchase success, a logic-based filtering system identified specific individuals who fit the "Buyer Sweet Spot" but had not yet made a purchase, culminating in a targeted Top 20 Lead List.

Key Findings

  • Lifestyle triggers; homeownership and short commute distances (0–1 miles) are the strongest predictors of purchase intent.
  • Friction points; high car ownership (3+ vehicles) and long commutes significantly decrease the likelihood of a bike purchase.
  • Target demographics; the "Middle Aged" and "Adult" segments with 0–2 cars provide the highest conversion stability.
  • Geographic opportunity; the Pacific and North American regions hold the highest concentration of high-income non-buyers, representing the best growth markets.

Future Growth

  • Time-series analysis; integrating historical sales dates to identify seasonal buying trends.
  • Predictive modeling; using the identified triggers to build a machine learning model that scores new leads as High, Medium, or Low priority.
  • CRM integration; automating the Top 20 list to refresh daily, giving the sales team real-time visibility of new high-value prospects.
