Insurance FAQ Assistant

A Python-based FAQ assistant that uses Streamlit and Pandas to navigate structured insurance datasets and provide instant, logic-based responses.

Python Streamlit Pandas Excel

Project Description

The Problem

Insurance companies manage large amounts of product data, policy rules, and procedures across multiple spreadsheets. Insurance teams rely on fragmented Excel-based systems to store policy rules and product information, resulting in slow retrieval times and inconsistent responses to customer queries.

The Solution

A Streamlit-based interactive assistant that transforms static insurance datasets into a structured decision system. By applying a rule-based decision tree combined with real-time Pandas filtering, users can instantly retrieve accurate policy information without manually searching spreadsheets.

Key Deliverables

  • Faster information retrieval; Reduces time spent searching across multiple spreadsheets.
  • Dynamic knowledge base; Enables non-technical updates via Excel.
  • Automated decision flow; Ensures consistent and accurate responses through structured logic.

Future Scalability

  • SQL migration; Designed for enterprise scalability with planned SQL migration to support larger datasets and multi-user environment.
  • Web deployment; Streamlit deployment enables integration into internal company portals for real-time customer and employee support.
